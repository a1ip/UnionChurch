Ministries

There are many ministry opportunities in Monterrey. The church is currently involved with many projects to help the community in need.
Assistance can include both financial and volunteer service, and some of these groups are:

  • El Rancho del Rey
  • Tesoros Escondidos
  • Asilo de Amor y Esperanza
  • Casa Hogar Hermosa Provincia
  • Casa Manantial de Amor
  • Asociación de Enfermeros

We need your help

The Union Church reaches out to various ministries within the metropolitan area of Monterrey, supporting them with prayer and financial gifts.

The Women’s Guild sponsors the annual Angel Tree. The tree has the names of children from several of the children’s homes. Members take the names to purchase a gift. Guild members plan Christmas parties for the homes and deliver the gifts.
